Common questions

Before You Book.

How often do I need to clean my hood?

Depends on how you cook. Solid fuel (wood, charcoal) is monthly. High-volume or 24-hour kitchens are quarterly. Most Long Island sit-down restaurants are semi-annual. Low-volume operations like churches or schools are annual. These are the NFPA 96 minimums. Your insurance carrier may require more.

How long does it take?

Most jobs run 3 to 6 hours. We schedule overnight or pre-open so your kitchen is ready when your first shift arrives. You shouldn't lose a single cover.

What's included?

Hood, plenum, filters, full accessible duct run, upblast fan, and rooftop containment. Plus before and after photos, a signed service report, and a dated NFPA tag on the hood.

Can you handle emergencies?

Yes. Failed inspections and last-minute cleanings get a 24-hour response anywhere on Long Island. We'll prioritize your job and get you back in compliance fast.

Do you work with my insurance?

Yes. Every clean comes with a signed service report and a dated metal tag. We send the documentation straight to you so you can forward to your carrier or fire marshal whenever they ask for it.
